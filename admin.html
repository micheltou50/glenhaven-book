🔄 iCal Calendar Sync

Your direct booking calendar is automatically exported for Airbnb to import. No manual action needed — Airbnb syncs every few hours.

Your iCal Export URL
https://glenhaven-book.netlify.app/calendar.ics

How to add to Airbnb:

  1. Go to your Airbnb listing → Calendar
  2. Click Availability Settings → Sync calendars
  3. Click Import Calendar and paste the URL above
  4. Airbnb will sync within a few hours automatically
