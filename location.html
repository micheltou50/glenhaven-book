Getting here

🚗 By car

Take the Western Motorway (M4) from Sydney. Journey time ~90 minutes. Free off-street parking for 2 cars.

🚂 By train

Blue Mountains Line from Central Station to Katoomba. Trains run hourly. Journey ~2 hours.

🚌 Local bus

Blue Mountains Bus Company connects Katoomba to major sights. Bus stop 3 min walk from property.

✈️ By air

Sydney Airport (SYD) is ~2 hrs away by car. Hire car recommended for exploring the mountains.

Our local picks

Curated by us — the places and walks we'd tell a friend about. All within easy reach of the cottage.

🥾
Walks and hikes
Three Sisters walk

The iconic lookout and walk — start at Echo Point, descend the Giant Stairway (800+ steps) through rainforest. Stunning.

⏱ 2-3 hrs▲ Moderate8 min drive
Prince Henry Cliff Walk

Spectacular clifftop trail connecting Echo Point to Gordon Falls Lookout. Multiple lookouts along the way.

⏱ 1.5 hrs▲ Easy-moderate10 min drive
Wentworth Falls circuit

Valley of the Waters — lush fern gullies, waterfalls, and a swimming hole at the base. Best after rain.

⏱ 3-4 hrs▲ Moderate-hard15 min drive
Grand Canyon track

Loop trail through a mossy slot canyon near Blackheath. Feels like another world. Family-friendly pace possible.

⏱ 3 hrs▲ Moderate20 min drive
Host tip

Start walks early — carparks at Echo Point fill by 10 AM on weekends. Pack layers; it's always a few degrees cooler on the cliff edge.
