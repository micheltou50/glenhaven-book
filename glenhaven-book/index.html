Our Story

A cottage that feels like home

"Tucked away on a quiet Katoomba street, Glenhaven was lovingly restored to preserve the warmth and character of its original timber bones — while adding every modern comfort a family could wish for."

Two storeys of thoughtfully designed space sleep 8 guests in comfort. Gather around the wood fireplace on cool mountain evenings, take your morning coffee in the sun-drenched conservatory, or let the kids loose in the sprawling private garden.

Everything you need is within reach — the Three Sisters, Echo Point, Katoomba's cafés and galleries, the Scenic Railway — all just minutes away.
