House Rules

Making your stay comfortable for everyone

We want Glenhaven to feel like home. These guidelines help us keep it that way for every guest.

Check-in & Check-out

Check-in
3:00pm
Self check-in via key lockbox
Check-out
10:00am

🏠 General

  • ✓ Maximum 8 guests at any time
  • ✓ Self check-in — lockbox code sent before arrival
  • ✓ Please treat the property as your own home
  • ✓ Report any damage or breakages promptly
  • ✓ Leave the property in a clean and tidy condition

🤫 Noise & Neighbours

  • ✓ Quiet hours: 10pm – 8am
  • ✓ No parties or large gatherings
  • ✓ Please be mindful of neighbours at all times
  • ✓ Music and outdoor entertaining welcome until 10pm

🚭 Smoking & Substances

  • ✗ No smoking inside the property
  • ✓ Smoking permitted in the garden only
  • ✗ No illegal substances on the premises
  • ✓ Please dispose of cigarette butts responsibly

🐾 Pets

  • ✓ Well-behaved pets welcome — please let us know
  • ✓ Pets must not be left alone in the property
  • ✓ Please keep pets off the furniture
  • ✓ Clean up after your pet in the garden

🔥 Fireplace & Safety

  • ✓ Wood fireplace available — firewood provided
  • ✓ Never leave fireplace unattended when lit
  • ✓ Extinguish fire fully before going to bed
  • ✓ Fire extinguisher located in the kitchen

🌿 Garden & Outdoors

  • ✓ BBQ available — please clean after use
  • ✓ Please do not pick plants or flowers
  • ✓ Gate must remain closed if travelling with pets
  • ✓ Outdoor furniture may be moved but please return it
💬 Questions before you arrive? We're always happy to help. Reach out via email at micheltou50@gmail.com and we'll respond promptly. We want your stay to be perfect.
